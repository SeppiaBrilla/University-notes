We define the projection of a linear space $V$ the [[linear mapping]] $\phi: V \rightarrow U$ where $U$ is a subpace of $V$ ($U \subseteq V$). $\phi$ must have the following property:
$$
\begin{align}
&\phi^2 = \phi \; \circ \; \phi = \phi  \\
&\Longrightarrow \\
&\phi(\phi(v)) = \; \phi(v) \\
&\forall \, v \in V
\end{align}
$$
