Standard IEEE

The IEEE standards for Floating-Point number are:

  • Single precision : 32 bit.
1 (sign) 23 (mantissa) 8 (exponent)
  • Double precision : 64 bit.
1 (sign) 52 (mantissa) 11 (exponent)

Due to the normaization the first bit is always 1 and, therefore, is hidden

Special values in the IEEE standards are:

  • Inf that occurs when dividing a finite number by 0 (like: 10\frac{1}{0}01​)
  • NaN for undefined or undetermined operations such as 00\frac{0}{0}00​ or 0 * Inf or InfInf\frac{Inf}{Inf}InfInf​
