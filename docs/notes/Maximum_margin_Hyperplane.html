Maximum margin Hyperplane

It is a method to find a separation between class for the Perceptron The maximum margin hyperplane method ensures the maximum separation between labels.

Convex hull

Is the tightest enclosing convex polygon of a set of points (the convex hull of two linearly separable classes do not intersect)

The maximum margin hyperplane is as far as possible to from both hulls and perpendicular to the shortest line connecting the hulls. In general, is sufficient a subset of the points of the hull. Those points are called support vector.
