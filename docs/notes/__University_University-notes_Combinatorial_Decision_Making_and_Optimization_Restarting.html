Restarting

It is a technique to avoid Heavy tail behaviour in Constraint solver algorithms. It restarts the search in a different branch after a certain amount of resources has been used. There are several ways to implement restart:

  • Constant (after L resources)
  • Geometric (after α∗\alpha^*α∗L resources, where * increases each time)
  • Luby: restarts after sL resources were consumed. s is the Luby sequence.
