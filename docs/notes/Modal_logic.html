Modal logic

A type of [Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence and Knowledge Representation/Module 2/Logic](Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence and Knowledge Representation/Module 2/Logic) built upon First order logic that adds the concept of "known" (a fact we know) and "believed" (a fact we have no certain)

K operator

The K operator is used to indicate that an agent knows something: KaPK_a PKa​P means that the agent a knows a fact P
