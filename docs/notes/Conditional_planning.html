Conditional planning

planners oftnen works under the assumption that the world is fully described in the state, but this is, of course, not true. To mitigate this problem, we can add a "sensing action" to the possible ones to sense the world and clear the uncertanties. A conditional planner is a Search strategies that generates various alternative plans for each source of uncertainty of the plan. It is constituted by:

  • casual actions
  • sensing actions for retrieving unknown information
  • several alternative partial plans of which, only one will be executed depending on the result of the observations.

Limitations

  • explosion of the search tree with high number of alternative contexts
  • a lot of memory consumed
  • alternative context are not always known in advantage -> often conditional planners are associated with probabilistic planners that plan only for the most probable contexts.
