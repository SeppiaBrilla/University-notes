Median filter

Median filter is a non-linear Image filters where each pixel is replaced with the median over a given neighborhood. Median filtering tends to keep sharper edges than linear filters such as Mean filter and Gaussian filter . It reduces Noise in the images because, often, the noisy pixels tend to have very high or very low values. Yet, Gaussian-like noise, such as sensor noise, cannot be dealt with by the Median, as this would require computing new noiseless intensities.
