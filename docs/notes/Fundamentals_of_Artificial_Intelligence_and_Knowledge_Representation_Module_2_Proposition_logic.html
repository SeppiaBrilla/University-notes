Proposition logic

It is a kind of Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence and Knowledge Representation/Module 2/Logic . In proposition logic, each symbol can be either true or fals, and they are called propositions or atoms or atomic formulas or Pi. Pi can be connected together with connectives: ∧,∨,→,¬,↔,⊥\wedge, \vee, \rightarrow, \neg, \leftrightarrow, \perp∧,∨,→,¬,↔,⊥

formulas that, no matter how we assing the value of the Pi, it will always be true (e.g. A∨¬AA \vee \neg AA∨¬A)
