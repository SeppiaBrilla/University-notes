Satisfiability Modulo Theory (SMT)

SMT concerns the study of the satisfiability of formulas with the respect to some theories (integer arithmetic, strings, array, ...). It extends SAT . There are two main approach to solve an SMT problem.

  • Eager approach : translates upfront the SMT formula in an equi-satisfiable SAT formula, and solves it with a SAT solver
  • Lazy approach : uses ad-hoc procedures for the background theory in a “lazy” way

Some interesting way we can use SMT:

  • EUF theory
  • Arithmetic theory
  • Array theory
  • Theory of bit-vectors
  • Theory of strings
  • LRA theory
  • LIA theory
  • Difference logic theory
