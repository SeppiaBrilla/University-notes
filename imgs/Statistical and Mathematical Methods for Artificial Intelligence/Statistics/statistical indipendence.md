two [[Random variable]] are statistically indipendent when:
$$
p(x,y) = p(x)p(y)
$$
([[Probability]], [[joint distribution]])

## consequences
$$
\begin{align}
p(y|x) &= p(y)\\
p(x|y) &= p(x)\\
\mathbb{V}_{X,Y}[x + y] &= \mathbb{V}_{X}[x] + \mathbb{V}_{Y}[y]\\
Cov_{X,Y}[x,y] &= 0\\
p(x,y|z) &= p(x|z)p(y|z) \; \forall z   
\end{align}
$$
